About Us
Our Story+x
So there I was, standing cluelessly in the grocery store aisle. “Which one of these cereals is healthier?” I thought to myself. How was I supposed to come to this conclusion? How am I supposed to know this stuff? I had to make a decision quickly because I was in a rush to get to my business meeting. A sudden rush of panic washed over me and I started grabbing cereals off the shelf at random. I sprinted through the checkout directly to my car, opened the door, and sat down in the driver seat with a sigh of relief. “Okay, let’s see what I got.” I thought to myself. I start opening grocery bags and pick up the cereal that I had just bought to take a better look at it. “What have I done….” I whispered to myself. I had grabbed “Sugar Puffs” in my panicked frenzy. I drove to my meeting feeling defeated. Not a single healthy choice was made that day. This is why we created FoodScore. To help our users make healthy decisions without having to know how to be healthy. No longer are the days of looking at those deceiving nutritional facts and ingredients. We have done all the research using trustworthy resources such as the Canadian National Food Guide, and Australian National Food Guide.
